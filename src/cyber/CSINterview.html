Cyber Security Interview Questions

General Cybersecurity Questions

1. What is the CIA Triad, and why is it important in cybersecurity?
2. Explain the difference between authentication and authorization.
3. What are the different types of firewalls, and how do they work?
4. What is encryption, and how does it protect data?
5. What is a VPN, and how does it enhance network security?
6. Can you explain the concept of multi-factor authentication (MFA)?

Tools and Technologies

1. What tools would you use for penetration testing?
2. What is an Intrusion Detection and Prevention System (IDPS)?
3. What is the role of Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) in cybersecurity?

Threats and Vulnerabilities

1. What is phishing, and how can you protect against it?
2. What is a Denial-of-Service (DoS) attack, and how can it be mitigated?
3. What is SQL injection, and how do you prevent it?
4. Explain the difference between a virus, worm, and Trojan.
5. What is ransomware, and what measures can be taken to prevent it?
6. What is a Man-in-the-Middle (MitM) attack, and how can it be prevented?

Cloud and Emerging Technologies

1. What are the main security risks in cloud computing?
2. How would you secure data in a cloud environment?
3. Explain the shared responsibility model in cloud security.
4. What is the role of Identity and Access Management (IAM) in cloud environments?
5. What are the cybersecurity implications of IoT devices?
6. How do you see the role of AI and machine learning in cybersecurity?
7. What are the potential cybersecurity risks of 5G technology?
8. How does blockchain contribute to cybersecurity?
9. What challenges does quantum computing pose to encryption?
10. What are some best practices for securing containerized applications (e.g., Docker)?
