Cybersecurity Roadmap

Understand the Basics(threats, vulnerabilities, and types of attacks.)
Networking Fundamentals(TCP/IP, networking protocols, and how data travels through networks.)
Learn About Vulnerabilities(SQL injection, cross-site scripting (XSS), and buffer overflow.)
Practice Ethical Hacking(Metasploit, Wireshark, and Nmap)
Security Operations & Incident Response(security incidents, and manage security tools.0
Advanced Security Concepts( encryption, penetration testing, and cloud security.)
