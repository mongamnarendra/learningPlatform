Mobile App Development Interview Questions

General Questions

1. What is the difference between native, hybrid, and web apps?
2. What mobile development frameworks are you familiar with?
3. What are the main differences between iOS and Android app development?

HTML/CSS/JavaScript Questions (for Hybrid Apps)

1.How do you create a responsive layout for mobile applications?
2. What are the best practices for optimizing performance in hybrid mobile apps?
3. How do you handle different screen sizes and resolutions in mobile apps?

Java Questions (for Native Android Development)

1. What is the Android Activity Lifecycle, and why is it important?
2. What is the Android Activity Lifecycle, and why is it important?
3. What are Services in Android, and when would you use them?
4. What is the purpose of Content Providers in Android?

Swift Questions (for iOS Development)

1. What are the key differences between Swift and Objective-C?
2. Explain the Model-View-ViewModel (MVVM) architecture.
3. What are the common ways to persist data in iOS applications?

Testing and Deployment Questions

1. What tools do you use for testing mobile applications?
2. How do you handle error logging and crash reporting in mobile apps?
3. What are the steps involved in publishing an app on the App Store or Google Play?

Backend and API Questions

1. How do you manage backend integration in mobile applications?
2. What are the common authentication methods used in mobile apps?
3. How do you ensure secure data transfer between a mobile app and the backend server?

User Experience Questions

1. What are the principles of Material Design?
2. How do you ensure accessibility in mobile applications?
3. How do you design a user-friendly interface for mobile apps?
